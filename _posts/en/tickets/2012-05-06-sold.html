To see the list of sold tickets for your event, go to the Sold tickets tab in your event details page and click See all.

The resulting page will display the selected performances and a list of sold tickets. This table can be sorted by clicking on the header cells. You may print this page (Ctrl + P on Windows, Cmd + P on Mac) and take it to the entrance to your event.

Excel report

The list of sold tickets can be opened with your favorite spreadsheet application. Click on the Excel report button at the top right corner, and when prompted give the file a .csv extension and save. If your event has specified any custom forms, the recorded data will be included in the generated report.

From your spreadsheet program (Excel, OpenOffice Calc) open the file and select the following:

  • Separator: commas
  • Character set: UTF-8

And click OK.
