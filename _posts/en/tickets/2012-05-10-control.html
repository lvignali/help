Security features

All tickets generated by Koliseo include three marks that are used to validate at the access control point:

  1. The ticket type: Indicates the type of ticket that was purchased.
  2. The amount

Tickets do not have to be printed to be valid; cell phones are equally valid to display a ticket. While validating tickets, you should just use the validation points described here.
