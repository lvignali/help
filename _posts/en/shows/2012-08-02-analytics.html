Google Analytics is a set of analysis tool to track traffic to your web pages. Koliseo is integrated with Google Analytics, which makes it possible top share traffic into your show pages for further analysis.

If you don't have a Google Analytics account you may create one for free here.

Configuring Google Analytics

To enable this feature just go to your profile page, click Edit and go into the Advanced tab. Introduce your Analytics Tracker ID into the field and click Save.

After saving you can check that it has been properly configured using the real-time reports in Google Analytics.

Recorded data

After your Google Analytics ID has been successfully configured any visit to your shows or user profile page will be recorded. You can choose how to take advantage of these statistics:

  • Offline ad campaigns (radio, TV) can be compared with the traffic flow to identify working patterns.
  • Track online sources such as blogs and social networks linking to your show page.
  • Correlate pricing to changes in your conversion rate.
  • Analyze the shows that are getting most attention and identify why.
  • Compare your weekly increase (or decrease) of traffic
You can get more information at the Google Analytics Help Center or contact us to get these reports as a customized service.
