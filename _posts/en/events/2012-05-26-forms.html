You can require all users to fill a form before getting their tickets. This is specified in the Form tab of the Event details page.

For every field you can specify the name, the type and wether it is required or not. The list of types available are Checkbox, Date, E-mail, Number, Phone, Text (a single line of text), Textarea (a multi-line text field) and Select (a field where you can choose from a series of predefined values). Select fields can specify a list of values separated by commas.
