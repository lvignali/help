What is the cost of using Koliseo?

Koliseo is free for event organizers. It operates on a fee charged over the ticket price that is paid with the ticket purchase. Everything else, including Koliseo Checkpoint Charlie for Android, is free.

What fee does Koliseo charge over the ticket price?

We charge 7% over the ticket price, with a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 15 of the event currency (EUR or USD). The following is an example in euros:
Ticket price Applicable fee
between 0.01 and 14 EUR1 EUR
between 15 and 214 EUR7% of the ticket price
above 215 EUR15 EUR

You can choose if you want to display this fee to the user during the ticket purchase process or not (fee paid by the organizer).

What about free events?

Free tickets are exempt of the minimum fee. If you are not making any income out of your event, we neither.

Which currencies are supported?

At the moment we support EUR and USD. Payments are executed in the same currency of the event.
