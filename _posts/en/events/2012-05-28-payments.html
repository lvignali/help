User balance

Koliseo keeps a balance with the accumulated income of any past performance in your account. This balance is available in the Balance tab in your user profile page, which also displays the list of past performances included in the balance. Once your balance gets above 100 EUR (or 100 USD) you can create a payment order to have this amount transferred to your bank account.

Payments

Payment orders (available in the Payments tab) are usually executed during the next five working days after creation. Clicking on each payment you can see the list of performances included in the payment.

In order to receive payment you must have introduced your payment data. On your user profile page click Edit and go to the Payment info tab. This can be filled in later, in which case you will receive a reminder once you have your first pending payment.
